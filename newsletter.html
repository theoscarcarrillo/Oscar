Spring 2026 by Oscar Carrillo

Oscar Carrillo is an aspiring cybersecurity analyst. The options in his head are endless, as he considers different avenues he could take in his career. Such as using skills learned for military service, Oscar has never been a violent person but could serve in a more technical way as he desires to serve for his country like many of his relatives have. Another possibility stems from some family connections as one of Oscar's uncles is a higher up in a security company that covers the whole country, everytime Oscar has visited his Uncle's house he is very impressed and wonders if that could be him one day.
Oscar has many interests but sports, technology, and pop culture are at the forefront of his passions. He has learned a lot about technology in his time at SCF such as Database designing, Programming, Computer information, Systems analysis and designs, Networking, Security, Server fundamentals, and even Web Page development. Oscar's favorite sports are baseball, basketball, and football with a strong interest in Texas sports since that is where his family originated from. Some pop culture icons that Oscar is a fan of include characters from the Marvel and DC comic universe as well as movie characters such as Indiana Jones and the people in Star Wars.
